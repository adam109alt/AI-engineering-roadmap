## What is context compaction? And what is the problem that LLMs have so we need context compaction?

## Solution:

1. Remove the "Fluff":

**Imagin if i said to you: *Please after you finish playing with your friends in the playstation clean you room*, Instead of saying all of that i can just say: *Clean your room after playing*, This reduce the memory to mutch**

2. Summarizing:

**If you gived the model a ver long story and you start to describe it word by word this will take to mutch memory, Instead you can just say the important things like: *The hero climbed the mountain and found the chess***

3. Picking the best parts:

**Sometimes our story have too mutch information that is unnecessary *Compaction* just give the model the important parts**

## Why we do all of this???

**Because the model memory space is limited, soo we can give to the model just the imporant things, and ignore the unimpotant things**
